# Image Generation Prompt

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 studio product photograph of an erhu-inspired two-string vertical fiddle prototype, emphasizing the active qianjin-to-bridge speaking span rather than total instrument length. Show a slim vertical neck, two strings, upper pegs, qianjin wrap/contact point, small bridge, resonator body with a membrane face, lower spike or support, and a bow resting between the strings. Materials should look like dark hardwood, light membrane, simple metal fittings, and a carefully adjustable prototype setup. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, realistic hand-held scale cues, about a 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop measurement scene of the erhu-inspired prototype held vertically at a bench, with a builder checking the qianjin-to-bridge contact span using a ruler while the bow sits between the two strings. Natural light, resonator and membrane visible, spare bridges and string-source notes nearby, concept-prototype tone without claiming traditional replica status.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of the qianjin wrap, two strings, and small bridge on the membrane face, showing the actual string contact points, adjustable bridge footprint, membrane texture, and bow hair passing between the strings with shallow depth of field.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, no watermarks, no violin-style fingerboard, no bow outside both strings, no invented cultural decoration, no impossible string path, anatomically plausible hands if shown.
